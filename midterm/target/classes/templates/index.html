Welcome to the Tae Kwon Do Student Checklist

This web application is designed to be used as a tool for my Tae Kwon Do school. It contains a list of students and their readiness for testing in a format that can be more easily updated.

Use the navigation bar above to access the other pages of the application.

Testing Rules

  1. Students must pay test fees before test day.
  2. Come on time.
  3. No talking during the test.
  4. Stay seated with your belt rank.
  5. Do not leave the dojang during the test. If you need to step out, wait for a break.
  6. No food or drinks allowed inside.
  7. The boards used in the test are 12" by 9" by 1" white pine (included in the test fee).
    • White Belt: 1 board
    • Yellow Belt: 1 board
    • Green Belt: 1 board
    • Green High Belt: 2 boards
    • Blue Belt: 2 boards
    • Blue High Belt: 3 boards
    • Brown Belt: 3 boards
    • Red Belts: 3+ boards
  8. Students must stay for the entire test. No leaving early. The tests are given every other month for white through brown belt. Speak to the instructor for the Black Belt test schedule. If you can't stay for the entire test, wait until the next test.
